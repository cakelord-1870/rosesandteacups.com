Victorian Tankards Porcelain Floral Mugs with Gold Trim USA Decorated

So much more than mere floral mugs, our Victorian Tankards and Floral Latte Mugs are oh so feminine.  They are hand decorated in the USA with amazing vivid florals using an artful process of decal application and kiln firing until the image becomes one with the porcelain just as if painted on!   You'll love our bone china floral mugs as well!  Great for wedding and housewarming gifts!  Be sure to visit our Gift Boxed Floral Mugs collection for mugs with gift boxes as pretty as what's inside!

Our floral mugs include pretty much any flower that you can think of!  From thistles to geraniums, ivy to iris, and daisies to daffodils, our garden is pretty full.  However, if you have a flower in mind that you don't see, we may well be able to get it for you, so be sure to ask!  

We offer several mug shapes including two styles of tankards, the standard Victorian Tankard which is very curvy, the Ladies' Victorian Tankard which is a little straighter, the latte mug with a trumpet shape, and the regular straight sided mug.
