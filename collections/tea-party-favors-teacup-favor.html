Tea Cup Tea Party Favors Perfect for Tea Parties Birthdays & Bridal Events

Our Teacup Favors begin with a lovely full size porcelain tea cup and saucer then add a tea bag of coordinating color, then gift wrap in cello and tie a ribbon on it with a bow around a demi tea spoon.  You'll be able to match the colors of your event from our many tea cup patterns and ribbon options. We have from one of our almost 25 patterns to select from on our Discount Teacups page

Our tea spoons come in a sweet and delicate size.  They are offered in silver plate, gold plate, and stainless steel with handles of tea cups, teapots and roses as well as our elegant traditional style.

Our tea cup favors look gorgeous on the table and are wrapped and ready to give.  They are a wonderful and lasting reminder of your special event.

For baby showers, we offer tea cup favors tied with baby feet ribbon and accented with tiny pink or blue pacifiers.

Our Roses and Teacups ladies make these favors at the time of your order at the Roses and Teacups warehouse in Arkansas, and you should see their hands fly during spring tea party season!  

We would love to help you choose just the right tea cup favors for your event.  Do you need something special?  Give us a call! 866-998-2877
