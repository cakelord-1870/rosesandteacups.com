Teapot Tea Cup Charms Sterling and Elegant Beads Swarovski Crystals

Teapot and Tea Cup Charms and Charm Bracelets in sterling, silver plate and gold vermeille using a variety of lovely beads including painted Czech beads, Japanese decal beads and Swarovski Crystal beads. Our tea charms are available in a variety of sizes to fit bracelets, pins and necklaces.

Some tips on teapot charm and jewelry size:

Our smallest beaded teapots are made with 10mm beads. They are the size that we typically use in our Teapot Earrings and Pins.

Our medium size teapots beads are 12mm. These are fine for charm bracelets or pendants, and are found on ourVictorian Dangle Necklaces. Our large beads are 14-16mm and work best for pendants.
