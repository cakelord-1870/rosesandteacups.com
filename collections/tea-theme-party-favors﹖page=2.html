  • Simple Tea Party Favor Display Ideas:
  • Tie a personalized teapot favor tag to a flavored tea spoon and place on the plate.
  • Place decorated sugar cubes in clear plastic wrap, tie with a bow and place in a tea cup.
  • Fan out children's tea cup and teapot lollipops on each plate.
  • Stuff a teapot partway with paper of plastic wrap and fill with flavored tea spoons like a floral bouquet.
  • Tuck a Tea Charm into our already filled tea party favor bags for extra fun!
  • Place a Purse Pad and an Elegant Pen on each napkin.
  • Place our decorative tea bags in one of our white boxes along with a tea wallet!
  • Place a decorated sugar cube in each tea spoon and tie on a favor tag.
  • Place a tea charm in each demi spoon.
  • Fill a crocheted lace tea cup with a small bag of candy or nuts.
  • Hang our Tea Ornament Favors from an Ornament Tree
  • Place boxes of tea soap favors on a separate table so that the scents don't interfere with your food fragrances.
  • Fill a tea wallet with our decorated tea bags and tuck in a teapot favor tag as you fold it.
  • Tie a Tea Charm onto a teacup handle with a teapot favor tag
